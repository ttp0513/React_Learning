# React Practice Exercises & Projects

This repo contains progressively challenging exercises for practicing **React components, props, state, lists, events, conditional rendering, forms, and hooks**.
Each section ends with **projects** that combine the concepts together.

---
- *** Make them look nice, use tailwind so you don't have to build out large stylesheets. ***

- *** Also side note, if you're having problems with styling. Look into both main.css and app.css at the root of your project. It comes with a good amount of predefined styles that are probably messing with whatever you're trying to do. ***
